Experience any city according to your interest



Xperio is a personlised experiences recommendation app.

Have you ever travelled to a new city and wish there is a friend who knows about you for years to recommend you places to go and things to try according to your interest and personality?

With Xperio, you’ll no longer have to spend hours online searching for that perfect place. We’ll show you hidden, unique, and independent gems, available at the tap of a finger. You can plan your day with itineraries based on your interests and location, designed to optimise your time and money. Basically, the only thing Experio can’t do is fly you there on a magic carpet. But don’t worry, we’re working on that.
