Experience any city according to your interest



KOMPAS is your virtual best friend for discovering the unique places hidden all around you. We all remember the storybooks of our childhood, detailing the adventures of Alice, Dorothy and Phileas Fogg. Who didn’t want to jump into the story and explore the worlds within the pages?

When we grow up and start exploring the real world, that sense of adventure and excitement often disappears. We get lost in our everyday lives and free time can become more of a routine than an adventure. It’s time to put an end to that and bring back the excitement.

With KOMPAS, you’ll no longer have to spend hours online searching for that perfect place. We’ll show you hidden, unique, and independent gems, available at the tap of a finger. You can plan your day with itineraries based on your interests and location, designed to optimise your time and money. Basically, the only thing KOMPAS can’t do is fly you there on a magic carpet. But don’t worry, we’re working on that.
